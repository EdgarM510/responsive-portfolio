About Me



Oh! hi... you gave me a scare, I didn't see you there.

What are you doing?

Huh? You'd like to know about me?

I dare not refuse, stay a while and listen.

My tale begins where it is likely to end, in a charming little city named Oakland, also known as Oaktown. Oaktown lays lazily on the side of a hill overlooking a beautiful and busy bay. I was made by two Mexican immigrants, born the youngest of four children. It was a scrappy upbringing, particularly in my younger years before moving up the hill.

For a couple of years I lived in San Jose, also known as Jotown. I still have many friends in Jotown and travel there often. I had several jobs after High School: Postal Worker, Liquor Store Clerk, Theatre Usher, Electrician, Solar Panel Installer, Auto-Detailer. Boring! let's talk hobbies: Yoga, Working out, Hiking, Drawing, Motorcycling, Esperanto, Cosplay, Overwatch, Table-Top Role Playing Games, Boardgames, Rubik's Cubing. I am freshly turned thirty, I'm studying to become a Full-Stack Web Developer and I would like to learn Mobile App Development next.

So yeah... that's pretty much it.

I'm done talking.

Get a move on.
